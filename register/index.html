Participation in all Linux Plumbers General Conference day and evening events and the Student Mini-Conference on Tuesday 9-16-08. You must be a full-time or part-time university student or an advanced high school student.

Portland is a city that bikes! Thursday night of the LPC is open to allow attendees to explore the city. A good way to do this is by bicycle! Please indicate on the registration form if you are interested in renting a bicycle for Thursday evening.
